Featured Projects

Illinois Space Society - Spaceshot GNC System

I attribute the majority of my technical knowledge to my favorite UIUC club. I spent 3+ years working as a GNC and FSW engineer, eventually becoming the GNC subteam lead in 2024-25. My interest in the cross-section of software and aerospace engineering began here. I would like to highlight a few projects I've worked on in my time:

Extended Kalman Filter (EKF)

My team and I developed our team's first EKF, designed for 6DOF state estimation. The filter was initially built and tested in our in-house Python simulator PySim to verify its behavior. After showing successful state-estimation, the algorithm was translated into C++ to be flown on our student-designed flight computer MIDAS.

SAM Turret

The SAM turret was our autonomous motor-mounted antenna turret tasked with tracking our rocket for telemetry that was developed under my leadership. The 3DOF PID motor control algorithm was implemented on a MIDAS board which connected to a motor board via ESPNow.

Tech Stack: Python, C++, Kalman Filters, Control Theory, Dynamics, Embedded Systems

Multi-Spacecraft Positioning for Interstellar Object Exploration

Developed a probabilistic multi-spacecraft deployment strategy that positions spacecraft to maximize visual information during interstellar object encounters using an uncertainty-aware cost function. Published research demonstrating optimal spacecraft terminal positions and points of interest selection.

Tech Stack: Python, MATLAB

DiffTune: Auto-tuning through Auto-differentiation

Implemented a gradient-descent based framework to efficiently tune parameters for controllers, leveraging automatic differentiation for optimization.

Tech Stack: Python, MATLAB, JAX, SciPy
